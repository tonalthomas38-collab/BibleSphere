📖 BibleSphere — Free Bible Study App

![Download](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-APK-green?style=for-the-badge)

- BibleSphere is a powerful and lightweight Bible study application designed to help believers explore Scripture deeply and meaningfully.
- It combines several essential Bible study tools into one simple mobile app including Bible versions, Strong's Lexicon, Nave's Topical Bible, Easton's Bible Encyclopedia, and Daily Blessings.
- BibleSphere is completely free, ad-free, and designed for serious Bible study.


🌍 Features

📖 Multiple Bible Versions
Read Scripture from multiple translations in an easy-to-navigate reader.

Features include:

- verse highlighting
- bookmarking
- copy verse
- share verse


🔍 Strong's Hebrew & Greek Lexicon

Study the original Biblical languages.

Search by:

- Strong's number
- Greek / Hebrew word
- transliteration meaning
- Includes alphabetical navigation for faster lookup.


📚 Nave's Topical Bible

Explore Scripture by topics and themes.

Features:
- topic search
- alphabetical topic index
- clickable verse references
- quick navigation to Bible reader
  

📘 Easton's Bible Encyclopedia

Understand Biblical places, people, and concepts with the classic Easton's Bible Dictionary.

Includes:
- alphabetical navigation
- instant search
- structured article view


✨ Daily Blessing

Grow spiritually every day.
Includes:

- 📖 Daily Bible Verse
- 🧠 Daily Bible Quiz
- Verses rotate automatically throughout the year.
